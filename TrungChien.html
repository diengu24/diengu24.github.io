Trung Chien (Vietnamese Fried Egg)




Ingredients:
  1. 4 eggs
  2. 1/2 onion sliced up
  3. 2 stems of green onion chopped up
  4. 1/2 tablespoon of fish sauce
  5. 1 teaspoon of sugar
  6. 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  7. 1 teaspoon of ground pepper
  8. 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  1. In a bowl, crack 4 eggs and add fish sauce, sugar, salt, green onions, and pepper and ix it all together.
  2. Heat up a skillet and put the oil in. Sautee the sliced onions in the oil. Pour in the egg mix and mix it with the onions.
  3. Cook until the egg is done
