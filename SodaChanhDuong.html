Soda Chanh Duong (Carbonated Lemonade)




Ingredients:
  1. 1 can of carbonated water
  2. A tablespoon of sugar
  3. Half a lemon
  1. Pour the carbonated water into a glass.
  2. Add sugar with the carbonated water.
  3. Squeeze in half a lemon.
  4. Mix everything together.
