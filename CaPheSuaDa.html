Ca Phe Sua Da (Vietnamese Iced Coffee)




Ingredients:
  1. 1/2 cup of drip coffee
  2. 2 tablespoons of condensed milk
  3. Cubed ice
  1. Add 2 tablespoons of condensed milk into the 1/2 cup of drip coffee.
  2. Then, add cubed ice.
  3. This will make one cup of ca phe sua da
