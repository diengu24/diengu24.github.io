Canh Chua (Sour Soup)




Ingredients:
  1. 1 slice of salmon, thorougly cleaned (Cut into 1 inch squares)
  2. 1 tablespoon of fishsauce
  3. 5 cups of water
  4. 2 teaspoons of white sugar
  5. 2 tablespoons of tamarind powder
  6. 1 teaspoon of salt
  7. 4 medium sized tomatoes
  8. 2 cups of mung bean sprouts
  9. 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  10. 2 cloves of minced garlic
  11. 2 stems of green onions chopped
  12. 1 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper
  13. Cilantro
  14. 1/4 pineapple
  1. Marinate the fish with fish sauce, pepper and onion for 15 minutes at room temperature.
  2. In a medium pot, put in oil, garlic, and 4 sliced tomatoes. Sautee them. Add 5 cups of water into the sauteed vegetables. When it boil, add in the marinated fish. Cook the soup for fifteen minutes on low heat. Use a mesh or small spoon to scoup out any scum that floats to the top.
  3. Add sugar, tamarind powder, and salt into the pot. Add the mung bean sprout and pineapple. Add salt for taste if needed.
  4. Turn off the stove and add cilantro and the green pepper.
