Chanh Muoi (Salty Lemonade)




Ingredients:
  1. Half of an aged salted lemon
  2. 1/2 cup of water
  3. 1/2 tablespoon of sugar
  4. Ice
  1. Pour 1/2 a cup of water into a glass.
  2. Add the sugar and the aged salted lemon.
  3. Mix everything together and add ice.
  4. This will make one glass of chanh muoi.
